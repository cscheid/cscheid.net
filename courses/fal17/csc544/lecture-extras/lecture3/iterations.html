From zero to visualizations in eight steps

  1. No reuse
  2. Generic element creator
  3. Loop over arbitrary data
  4. getter functions for specific attributes
  5. use getters of iteration 4 to adjust visualizations
  6. getter generators, higher-order functions
  7. generic plotAll for any SVG element, just like generic make in iteration 2
  8. Three different visualizations, each with 10 lines of code
